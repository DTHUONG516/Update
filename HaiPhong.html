    Hai Phong city

    Hai Phong is an important port city, industrial center, and the largest seaport in the North of Vietnam, as well as the economic, cultural, medical, educational, scientific, commercial and technological center of the country. Northern coastal region. This is the third largest city in the country, the second largest in the North after Hanoi

    Tourist Attraction

    • Flamingo Cat Ba Beach Resort
      Cat Co Beach 1-2, Cat Ba Town, Cat Hai District, Hai Phong City
    • Proudly located on Lan Ha Bay - the top most beautiful bays on the planet, the Flamingo Cat Ba resort complex appears as a green masterpiece on a pearl island. Immersed in the majestic nature and unique hanging garden landscape, visitors will have unforgettable moments and experiences.
    • Price: from 3.450.000. VND/night
    • Holiday Hon Dau Resort
      Area 3, Van Huong Ward, Do Son District, Hai Phong City
    • It is a continuous complex with entertainment resorts, restaurants, hotels and resort villas meeting international standards. One-stop ecosystem - visitors will be able to explore famous miniature landmarks of Vietnam along with high-class service quality.
    • Price: from 900.000 VND/night
    • Cat Ba Island Resort & Spa
      Cat Co 1, Cat Ba Town, Cat Hai District, Hai Phong City
    • Cat Ba Island Resort & Spa, ranked as one of the best hotels in Catba, Cat Ba Island Resort & Spa is a the best option when looking for a Catba beach resort. With its rich dark woods, well-appointed ocean view accommodations and attentive staff, a stay at the resort provides casual luxury.
    • Price: From 900.000 VND/night
    • Airway system
    • Hai Phong Airport or Catbi Airport (HPH), an international airport located 5 km from the city center. Cat Bi Airport was built by the French during the French colonial period
    • now hosts domestic flights from Ho Chi Minh City, Phu Quoc, Pleiku, Nha Trang, Da Lat and Da Nang and also international routes that connects Macau, Seoul and Hai Phong. JetStar Pacific, VietJet Air, Vietnam Airlines and Emirates are some airlines which are exploiting Catbi airport.
    • Hai Phong railWay
    • Hanoi - Hai Phong railway is a railway connecting the capital Hanoi with the port city of Hai Phong.
    • This railway line is 102 km long, passing through 4 provinces and cities: Hanoi, Hung Yen, Hai Duong and Hai Phong. It is an "edge" of the economic development triangle (Hanoi - Hai Phong - Quang Ninh) of northern key economic region. The starting point is Hanoi station and the ending point is Hai Phong station
    • Highway system
    • Hanoi - Hai Phong Expressway (symbol of the entire route is CT.04, also known as National Highway 5B) is a highway section of the Vietnam highway system in Northern Vietnam, the This highway is part of the Trans-Asia route
    • Hai Phong Opera House
      28 Ward Tran Hung Dao, Hoang Van Thu, Hong Bang, Hai Phong
    • Hai Phong Opera House is officially called the City Theater and is the place to organize major cultural and political activities of Hai Phong. The City Theater is considered a famous symbol of the port land when it witnessed many important revolutionary events of Hai Phong city and the country. With special values, Hai Phong Opera House is recognized as a national Architectural and Artistic relic.
    • Hai Phong Museum
      66 Dien Bien Phu Ward, Minh Khai, Hong Bang, Hai Phong
    • Hai Phong Museum was built in 1919 on a vast 1-hectare area beneath the shade of century-old trees. This European Gothic building used to serve as the France-China Bank during the French colonial period.
    • Dinh Vu _Cat Hai sea crossing bridge
      The connection point from the Hanoi - Hai Phong expressway (at Tan Vu node) is in Trang Cat ward, Hai An district; The end point is Lach Huyen port (Hai Phong international gateway port) in Cat Hai district.
    • The longest sea-crossing bridge in Vietnam and one of the longest sea-crossing bridges in Southeast Asia. The bridge across the sea is 29.5m wide with 4 lanes (2 lanes for motor vehicles and 2 lanes for non-motorized vehicles). The bridge is designed to run at a speed of 80 km/h.
    • Do Son Beach
      Do Son tourist area, Van Huong, Do Son, Hai Phong
      Do Son Beach possesses the characteristics of the Northern Sea, which is cloudy water and brown sand. The beach is divided into 3 main areas: area 1 has many rocks so it is difficult to swim, area 2 attracts many tourists, while area 3 is quiet, has few services but is ideal for those who like peace.
    • Cat Ba Island
      Cat Ba island district, Hai Phong
      Cat Ba archipelago is considered the Pearl Island of port land. This place has majestic beauty and still retains its wildness with limestone mountains, long beaches, and blue sea water.
