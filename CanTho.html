    Can Tho

    Can Tho is one of six centrally run cities in Vietnam. This is the busiest and most developed city in the Mekong Delta, serving as the economic, cultural, social, medical, educational and commercial center of the entire region. Can Tho is currently a grade I urban area, a central city at the regional and national level.

    • Legacy Mekong
      Con Au, Cai Rang, Can Tho
    • Legacy Mekong Can Tho is located on Con Au - a lush green island on the banks of the Hau River in the Mekong Delta. Legacy Mekong Can Tho is designed with harmonious architecture. This is a combination of traditional Vietnamese style and modern interior. There are a total of 60 rooms and 5 pool villas. The entire resort uses traditional local materials
    • Price: from 5.900.000 VND/night
    • Victoria Can Tho resort
      Cai Khe Ward, Ninh Kieu District, Can Tho City, Vietnam
    • Victoria Can Tho Resort is known as a green oasis in the heart of Can Tho city, with a blend of delicate Indochina architecture and charming natural beauty of the river. The resort owns a green campus with two swimming pools overlooking the river and world-class resort amenities.
    • Price: from 2.940.000 VND/night

    • Airway system
    • Can Tho Airport is located along Hau Giang River, the east end of this airport is 700m from Hau River, 500m to the north from Tra Noc canal, and to the west is Ba Ly canal. The airport connects to Can Tho city center by Vo Van Kiet and Le Hong Phong streets
    • The passenger terminal's architecture comes from the idea of ​​a dinghy of the Mekong Delta and can be expanded for the next stages of operation when the terminal exceeds forecast capacity.
    • Bus system
    • Can Tho City's public bus transportation system has undergone a dramatic transformation. Can Tho City continues to expand its bus network, invest in bus infrastructure development, develop digital technology.. to help people access public passenger transport services more easily and conveniently.
    • Can Tho currently has 7 intra-provincial bus routes operating without subsidies, 10 adjacent bus routes, of which 7 routes are in operation.

    • As a river city, Can Tho has a very developed waterway and ship transportation system.     • Ninh Kieu wharf
      106 Hai Ba Trung Street, Tan An, Ninh Kieu, Can Tho
    • A water wharf and a tourist and cultural landmark of Can Tho city formed in the 19th century. Ninh Kieu Wharf is located on the right bank of Hau River, between the confluence of Hau River and Can Tho River, adjacent to Hai Ba Trung Street, Tan An Ward, Ninh Kieu District, Can Tho City.
    • Munir Ansay Temple
      2QMM+CH7, 36 Hoa Binh street, Tan An, Ninh Kieu, Can Tho
    • Munir Ansay is a sacred temple, famous for its unique Khmer architecture. The main items include the main hall, the monks' buildings, the kitchen, the reception hall, the tower to keep the ashes of the dead, the Xa Loi tower and the main gate which is elaborately carved according to the way of worshiping the gods. of the Khmer people.
    • Binh Thuy ancient house
      144 Bui Huu Nghia Street, Binh Thuy, Binh Thuy, Can Tho
    • The ancient house has a unique interference between Eastern and Western architectural imprints. Ancient houses have mysterious stories about feng shui talismans. There is still a story circulating that somewhere in this house there is a feng shui talisman. It is this talisman that has helped the Duong family prosper for many generations.
    • Cai Rang floating market
      Can Tho river, near Cai Rang bridge, about 7 km from Can Tho city center.
      Cai Rang floating market is a wholesale floating market specializing in buying and selling fruits and vegetables on Can Tho river and is a unique tourist attraction of Cai Rang district, Can Tho city.
    • Bang Lang stork garden
      7GJ4+J66, Thuan An, Thot Not, Can Tho.
      This stork garden stands out with its large space, surrounded by mangrove forests and green rice fields of the Mekong Delta. The special feature of Bang Lang stork garden that makes many Can Tho tourists excited is the presence of wild storks, living naturally in the natural environment of this river land.
    • Military Region 9 Museum
      6 Hoa Binh Street, Tan An, Ninh Kieu, Can Tho
      The Museum of the Armed Forces of Military Region 9 is a type of Military History Museum, located in the system of Museums of the Vietnam People's Armed Forces and the Ho Chi Minh Museum Mekong Delta branch is a type of museum. The Museum of Famous People is part of the system of Museums and Memorial Monuments of President Ho Chi Minh, with the task of researching, collecting, inventorying, preserving, displaying, propagandizing and introducing the process of construction and war.
